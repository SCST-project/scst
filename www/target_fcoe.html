FCoE target

SCST Fibre Channel over Ethernet (FCoE) target is developed by Open-FCoE team and Joe Eykholt. Since February 2010 the main development place of it is SCST SVN repository.

It is on the preliminary stage. You can download it from the SCST SVN repository. See the download page how to setup access to it.
